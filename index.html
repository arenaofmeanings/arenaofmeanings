ARENA OF MEANINGS
takes on the open ended structure of the internet, made of digital dispersions and swarm circulations.

An anarchy of meanings, an arena where everything can be morphed into something else almost as a telephone game, in which the original message is disseminated and contaminated unfolding an unexpected narrative throughout the way.

This is one of an infinite amount of possible different narratives. It can be continued, changed or re-constructed.
